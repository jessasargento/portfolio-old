About Me

Hi! Welcome to my mini portfolio site. My name is Jessa, but I prefer to be called as Jssea (pronounced as je-se-ya). I am a BA Film graduate from the University of the Philippines Diliman. My interests include filmmaking, graphic design, and multimedia arts. Throughout my undergraduate years in the university, I served my organizations as a creative member, in charge of publications and visual outputs. Since then, I have learned many techniques when it comes to designing and layout.

In 2018, I directed and edited my own short feature entitled “Once Lang Magkafirst Love”. It has won awards from several student film competitions around NCR such as CineMapua 2019, bagging Cardinal Gold Film Award and other four awards and Indie Un-Film 2019.

In my spare time, I like to watch horror films and make myself cry with sad movies and k-dramas. I also enjoy photography, music, writing, cooking, baking, and traveling. Here's how to reach out to me.
