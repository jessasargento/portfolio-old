About Me

Hi! Welcome to my mini portfolio site. My name is Jessa, but I prefer to be called Jssea (pronounced as je-se-ya). I am a BA Film graduate from the University of the Philippines Diliman. I am interested in filmmaking, writing, graphic design, and multimedia arts. Throughout my undergraduate years in the university, I served my organizations as a creative member, in charge of publications and visual outputs. Since then, I have learned many techniques when it comes to designing and layout.

In 2018, I directed and edited my own short feature entitled “Once Lang Magkafirst Love”. It has won awards from several student film competitions around NCR such as CineMapua 2019, bagging Cardinal Gold Film Award and awards for directing, screenplay, and production design, and Indie Un-film Festival 2019, winning the Special Jury Prize award.

In my spare time, I like to watch horror films and make myself cry with sad movies and k-dramas. I also enjoy photography, music, writing, cooking, baking, and traveling. Here's how to reach out to me.
